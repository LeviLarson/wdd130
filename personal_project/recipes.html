Protein Pancakes

    Ingredients

  1. 1 cup of oats
  2. 1 banana
  3. 2 eggs
  4. 1/2 a cup of egg whites
  5. 1 tsp baking powder
  6. a pinch of salt
  7. 1 - 2 scoops of protein powder
  8. 2 tablespoons flax meal

Instructions

Run everything through the blender on medium low speed until very well mixed. Heat a nonstick griddle to medium high heat. Add batter in small circles â€“ about 1/4 cup per pancake. Sprinkle with blueberries or chocolate chips if you want. When the edges start to look dry (2-3 minutes), flip and cook another minute or two on the other side. Top with anything you like! I like syrup and peanut butter and chocolate chips.

Protein Ice Cream

    Ingredients

  1. Frozen fruit of your choice
  2. Milk of your choice
  3. Protein Powder
  4. Vanilla extract (for a little extra flavor)

Instructions

Place all ingredients in a food processor or Vitamix. Blend until smooth and creamy. I blend this on low then pulse until it has the texture of soft-serve ice cream. You can enjoy it immediately or freeze it for 1-2 hours in a sealed container for a more traditional ice cream texture
